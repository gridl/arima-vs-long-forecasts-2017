ARIMA (simple form)

$$ y'_{t} = c + \phi_{1}y'_{t-1} + \cdots + \phi_{p}y'_{t-p} \\ + \theta_{1}e_{t-1} + \cdots + \theta_{q}e_{t-q} + e_{t} $$
